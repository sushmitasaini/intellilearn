What is Cognitive Services


Cognitive services helps in treatment of the injury related to brain. Such problems relate to the problem of thinking and behavior.
Cognitive services include the services related to the
  • Assessment of the person’s need
  • Behavioral therapy. Behavioral therapy involves the counseling of the individual with his/her family.Cognitive services take place usually in professional setting.

What is Microsoft

Microsoft Corporation, commonly referred to as Microsoft is an American multination technology company, that develops, manufactures, licenses, supports and sells the computer software, consumer electronics and personal computers and services. Its best known software products are the Microsoft Windows line of operating systems, Microsoft Office office suite, and Internet Explorer and Edge web browsers.

What are Microsoft cognitive services?


Cognitive services have four categories:
  • A.Applications program interface
  • B.Pricing
  • C.Applications
  • D.Developers

APPLICATIONS PROGRAM INTERFACE


A.Vision
  • Computer vision API –Distill actionable information from images.
  • Emotion API–Personalize experiences with emotion recognition.
  • Face API–Detect, analyze, identify, organize and tag faces in photos.
  • Video API–Analyze, edit, and process videos within your app.

B.Speech
  • Bing speech API–Convert speech to text and back again, and understand its intent.
  • Custom recognition intelligent service–Fine-tune speech recognition for anyone, anywhere.
  • Speaker recognition API–Give your app the ability to know-who’s talking.

C.Language
  • Bing spell check API–Detect and correct spelling mistakes with your app.
  • Language understanding intelligence services–Teach your app to understand the command from the users.
  • Text analytics API–Detects sentiments, key phrases, topics, and languages from your text.

D.Knowledge
  • Academic knowledge API–Explore the relationships among academic papers, journals and authors.
  • Entity linking intelligence service –Contextually extend knowledge of people, location and events.
  • Knowledge exploration service –Add interactive search over structured data to your project.
