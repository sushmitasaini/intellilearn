Technology Tutorials:Snapchat Uncover Discover

Posted By:Sakshi Jain

  • Share on Facebook
  • Tweet
  • Share on Google+
Snapchat is the best combination of social network, magazines and televisions in the redesigned format.
Now you can see the image and headlines preview of the content inside the discover channels and live stories on the stories pages instead of just the logos of the publishers or the events they capture.



Instead of digging the favorite channel from the whole list you can now tap and hold the story u like and subscribe them and they then almost appear amongst the recent stories unless u unsubscribe it .The goal is of making the content more professional and community curated so that it can be attractive to the users like other apps.



The discover redesign should be that it provides the profitable deal for publishers. Without any previews, the channel buttons looks same all day and was not interesting that might have reduced the potential viewership for the channels whose cost brands a lot.
But now with eye-catching peeks at what’s inside, users may be more willing to click-through and watch the combination of videos, text articles and ads featured within discover channel.


Since Snapchat splits the ad revenue from inside discover channels with their publishers, its starting earning money from their viewership
