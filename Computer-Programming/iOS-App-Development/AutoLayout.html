iOS App Development Tutorials:Autolayout in iOS App Development

Posted By:Himanshu Goyal

  • Share on Facebook
  • Tweet
  • Share on Google+
Auto Layout is a way that lets developers create user interface by defining relationships between elements. It provides a flexible and powerful system that describes how views and the UI controls relate to each other. By using Auto Layout, you can get an incredible control over layout, with a wide range of customization, and yield the perfect interface. Auto Layout is a fantastic tool. It does things that earlier technologies could never dream of. Okay let us consider an example to understand the concept of AutoLayout Here I have bulid a view that consist of the label and a textview and a button. Now I will tell you how to use AutoLayout.   • Here the meaning of (Leading Space to Container) means the space from the left side boundary of the View.
  • The meaning of (Trailing Space to Container) means the space from the right side boundary of the View.
  • The meaning of (top Space to Container) means the space from the top boundary of the View.
  • The meaning of (Bottom Space to Container) means the space from the bottom boundary of the View.
  • The meaning of (Centre Horizontally in Container) means if we want to place the item horizontally at the centre of the view.
  • The meaning of (Centre Vertically in Container) means if we want to place the item vertically at the centre of the view.
