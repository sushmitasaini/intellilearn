iOS App Development Tutorials:What is iOS 9

Posted By:Gaurav Saini

  • Share on Facebook

  • Share on Google+
With the release of iOS 9, the Apple has reached to new heights. With the launch of new apps and of course the brand new OS X version. But the main and amazing thing that apple has launched at this year WWDC is iOS 9.It has become the habit of the Apple to launch the new version of the iOS at the WWDC. For the past few month it was a rumour that the apple will launch its app with greater UI and with some new feature. And truly Apple has done that some of the existing apps has got better refinement which was needed. But overall the apple has added some new feature to There apps, some of them were unexpected. The more you do with iOS 9 the more You will happen to understand it

Improved Siri With Proactive

Siri, the Apple official human talking interface has been improved a lot with the indulgement of the proactive with it. With the release of the Google now feature on the android by the Goggle, it was necessary for the apple to improve his voice query feature in its iPhone, and yes Apple has certainly done that. With the indulgement of the Proactive with the Siri, the app has been certainly improved. If for example we say that “some my all Photos from the last trip” the Siri will show that at that instance. Apple‘s involvement of the Proactive with the Siri has Lead to a greater changes in Siri. With the advancement of the siri, the Siri will now optimise your phone use depending upon the type of the Activity that we are doing. <>
Siri will tell us about our meeting on the day and will alert in advance. This is all bundled in the new UI.

Apple Music

The apple has launched the music app at $9.99 monthly and $14.99 for the family plan. With the launch of the apple’s music app the apple has given direct competition to the existing music apps like Spotify and rdio. Apple has provided a free 3 month Trail period so that the user can take a test of the app before purchasing the full version. Apple’s new service looks like it will offer some big advantages over other options, including native integration with the iOS music app itself. It features curated playlists, too, as well as special collections curated by top artists.


Maps

Apple has really worked hard on There map Application. The new map application will now feature the transit system that will also give us the information about the local transportation available. The map feature will give the whole information about the whole journey and the route it will also contain the information of the restaurant, and other recreation places on the route. The maps s launched for few of the cities. When you search in map you will see the nearby places, on the list you will see the most popular restaurants, shopping and more. Like when you search for restaurant under the food category, it will show all the popular restaurant, bakeries, fast food corner, cakes store and other popular places in your area based on your locality.


CARPLAY

An Amazing app that apple has given to the car owners is CARPLAY. This app will let the user to listen the voice Messages letting you the Hear the messages in the sender’s original voice. But this app wok if your car support the wireless communication. Carplay is a smarter way of using the iPhone in the car. CarPlay takes all the thing that you want to do with your iPhone while you are driving whether you want to listen to the music, you want to receive the calls, make calls and more importantly to just focus on the driving

An Amazing app that apple has given to the car owners is CARPLAY. But this app wok if your car support the wireless communication. Carplay also contain the Siri voice control feature that enables the things you want to do. CarPlay also works with the Knobs and the buttons and makes it very easy to just focus on the road.

Android Migration

Sometime it becomes necessary to move the data the from your old android phone to the new iPhone. For this the apple has launched the new Application that is Android Migration.it transfers all the important details with the greater security, it can transfer all the free apps that you use in the android phones along with the information stored in them.

For doing this you need to download the move to iOS application to wirelessly move the application details from the android phone to the new IPhone or iPad. And the paid app are added to the iTunes wishlist.

Security

In this year WWDC apple has majorly focused in the security of the Application. With the release of the iOS 9, apple has introduced greater security to There application. The apple has provided more security feature to the application to securely store the user personal information and the Apple ID. iOS 9 provide more advance security by increasing the strength of the passcode that protects the device and making it difficult for others to get unauthorised access to the Apple ID. These security features are easy to use for the user while it is difficult for unauthorised users to get access to any information. For doing this you need to download the move to iOS application to wirelessly move the application details from the android phone to the new IPhone or iPad. And the paid app are added to the iTunes wishlist.

SDK

The apple provides new API that gives the developer a chance to enable the new functionality in there application. Multitasking and gaming API help the developer and the gamer to create some new and amazing things. The amazing application carplay, improved search option with Siri are some of the new features of the iOS 9 Well the Apple has used its slogan “Think Different”in a pretty good manner.
