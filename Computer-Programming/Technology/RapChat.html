Technology Tutorials:What is Rapchat

Posted By:Sakshi Jain

  • Share on Facebook
  • Tweet
  • Share on Google+
Rapchat is the rap app in the app store, that let you create, discovers the rap all from your phone. It connect you to the 100’s producers that provide background music to i.e. free beats and upcoming instrumental beats that help you to make the rap.

In the latest fusion of music and social media, a new app has emerged that allows users - mostly college and high school students - to quickly share their freestyle raps.

Things that can be appreciated.

  • App gets started quickly and you can record right away.
  • The app is simple to use.
  • The app has the feature that is you can either sing in the mic or you can either write to make the rap.
  • You have the options to record the rap through speakerphone or while wearing the headphones.
  • You can directly send the link to people who don’t have the app to listen to it.
